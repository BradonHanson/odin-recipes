Scratch Brownies

Description

These Delicious Brownies are some of the simplest to make and will have you coming back for more.

Using only six ingredients and taking just a little over an hour to make this might just save your dinner party after a busy day or really scratch that chocolate craving without too much effort.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated suger
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Heat Oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat an 8x8-inch metal baking dish with butter.

  2. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes. Whisk in cocoa powder, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir in eggs, one at a time until incorperated. Add flour and stir until combined. Scrape mixture into prepped pan and smooth the top.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.

  4. Remove from the oven and cool on a wire rack for at least 45 minutes before cutting.
