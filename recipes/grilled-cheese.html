Grilled Cheese

Description

A good grilled cheese sandwich is a simple grilled cheese sandwich. Using some bread, a cheese of your choice, and some butter to make the magic happen, a tasty lunch or snack is easy and quick.

Ingredients

  • White Bread (or any lighter bread)
  • Cheese (American recommended)
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Heat frying pan to medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of two slices of bread.
  3. Add cheese slices to UNBUTTERED sides of bread and press together so the cheese is between the bread and the butter is on the outside.
  4. Lay sandwich on hot frying pan and let cook until cheese is melted and bread is toasted, flipping every 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with tomato soup.
